Horse Coaching

At EquiTrain, we understand that every horse is unique. Our horse coaching program is designed to cater to each horse’s unique personality and learning style, helping them reach their full potential and excel in their chosen discipline. Our coaches are experienced and knowledgeable, with a deep understanding of horse behavior and training techniques. We use positive reinforcement techniques to build trust and rapport with our equine partners, ensuring they enjoy the learning process and are motivated to excel. Our program is tailored to each horse’s needs and goals, whether it’s improving their dressage movements, jumping technique, or general fitness. With EquiTrain’s horse coaching program, you can be sure that your horse is in good hands and on the path to success.

Rider Training

At EquiTrain, we believe that a successful partnership between horse and rider is built on a foundation of mutual trust, communication, and skill. Our rider training program offers comprehensive instruction tailored to each rider’s skill level and goals, helping them improve their technique and confidence in the saddle. Our coaches are skilled and experienced, with a deep understanding of rider biomechanics and horse behavior. We use proven techniques to help riders develop their balance, coordination, and timing, ensuring they communicate effectively with their equine partner. Whether you’re a beginner or an advanced rider, our rider training program can help you achieve your goals and take your riding to the next level.

Equine Education

At EquiTrain, we believe that successful horsemanship requires not only practical skills but also a deep understanding of horse behavior, anatomy, and health. Our equine education program provides a comprehensive approach to horsemanship, covering everything from horse anatomy and nutrition to first aid and emergency preparedness. Our instructors are experienced and knowledgeable, with a passion for sharing their expertise with others. We offer a range of courses and workshops tailored to different levels of expertise and interest, from basic horsemanship to advanced veterinary care. Whether you’re a new horse owner or a seasoned equestrian, our equine education program can help you deepen your knowledge and become a better horse person.

Horse Boarding

At EquiTrain, we understand that finding the right boarding facility for your horse can be a stressful and time-consuming process. Our horse boarding services provide a safe and comfortable home away from home for your equine partner, with top-notch care and amenities to ensure their well-being. Our boarding facility is staffed by experienced and knowledgeable professionals who are dedicated to providing the best care for your horse. We offer a range of services and amenities, from daily stall cleaning and turnout to veterinary care and grooming. With our horse boarding services, you can rest assured that your horse is in good hands and receiving the best care possible.
